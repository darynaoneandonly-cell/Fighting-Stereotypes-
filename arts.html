You are not alone.

Eating disorders are one of the deadliest mental illnesses around the world. Therefore, it is not a condition that it is to be joked about or treated lightly. To be honest, I did not know who to trust or to rely on when I was confused about what is happening. Professionals might be sometimes out of reach, however I realised that there is always somebody you can talk to. I personally went into research to find many contact numbers around the world that you can call or text freely when you are unsure about your mental condition.
